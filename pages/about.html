About



ALL RIGHTS RESERVED is an interactive web project using p5 js, html, and css to satirize some of the major criticisms I have surrounding copyright laws.

I wanted to emphasize some of the negative effects around copyright in a humorous way by taking the user through a story where they lose their rights and navigate through overcomplicated hurdles that parallel the exploitative tactics used in the real world.
